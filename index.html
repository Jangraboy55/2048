How to Play

Use your arrow keys or WASD to move the tiles.

On touch devices, swipe in the direction you want to move the tiles.

When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

After each move, a new tile (value 2 or 4) appears on the board.

Try to create a tile with the value 2048 or even higher!

Tips & Strategies

Keep your largest tile in a corner

Try to arrange tiles in descending order

Use undo strategically to correct mistakes

Plan several moves ahead
